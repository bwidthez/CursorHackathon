Your rights and next steps

(Preview only — run the app with npm to get real AI advice.) Under the Renters' Rights Act 2025, your landlord cannot evict you without a court order. They must give you proper written notice and use a valid ground. If they have only given you two weeks verbally, that is not sufficient. Do not leave the property. Contact West Northamptonshire Council to report if you are being pressured, and consider speaking to Shelter or Citizens Advice.
